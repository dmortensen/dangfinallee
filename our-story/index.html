Our Story

We thought our story didn’t start until college, but evidence shows...

Circa 2002

June 2007

He tells his boys he’s crushing on her.

December 2007

He buys her 3 bags of Trader Joe’s banana crisps for a retreat.

December 2007

She received 3 bags of Trader Joe’s banana crisps (Boo! They are discontinued!) without asking, and took it as a sign this boy might like her...

July 2008

He was going to tell her he had feelings for her but chickens out.

August 2008

Even after driving down to Disneyland to support her first ½ marathon, he chickens out again.

September 2008

He tells her he likes her (finally).

November 2008

He tries to take her to the Academy of Sciences for their first date, but didn’t reserve tickets.

November 2008

She thinks, “I told you so...”

June 2009

She graduated from Cal Poly SLO and packed her belongings back home to Castro Valley, CA.

July 2009

She accepts her first post-grad job at Intuit, working with former classmates.

December 2009

They drive to Los Angeles, CA for their first Disneyland trip together.

November 2010

She makes a switch and works for LEVEL Studios (or is it Rosetta? No, it’s LEVEL.)

November 2011

He painfully walks his first and very last half marathon in the Big Sur Half Marathon.

November 2011

She runs her best personal record in the Big Sur Half Marathon.

August 2012

She makes San Jose, CA her new home.

January 2013

He knows she’s the one.

November 2013

He gets his first taste of the tech world at Google.

October 2014

For the first time, she joins in on his family vacation in Maui, HI.

May 2015

He goes from watching movies to helping stream movies at Netflix.

June 2015

The Golden State Warriors are the Champions of the World.

August 2015

She takes the leap of faith and joins team Apple.

November 2015

He picks out THE diamond.

November 2015

He reveals to his boys his plan for proposing.

November 2015

She vents to girlfriends that she is antsy for a ring.

December 2015, 3:30 am

He rehearses what he plans to say when he proposes.

December 2015, 5:30 am

They drive 26 miles for a morning hike.

December 2015, 7:30 am

At 3 miles long and 2,000 feet in elevation, they make it to the top of Mission Peak.

December 2015, 7:35 am

While huffing and puffing, he speaks some gibberish and then gets down on one knee to ask if she’ll marry him.

December 2015, 7:37 am

She says “Yes”.
